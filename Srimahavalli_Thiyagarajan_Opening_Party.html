Your Introduction

Bharatanatyam artist Srimahavalli Thiyagarajan delves deep into the classical vocabulary of this ancient South Indian form, combining technical precision with emotional depth to communicate the profound spiritual and aesthetic dimensions of the tradition.
About you
I am Srimahavalli, from Seychelles and currently a second-year International Business Management student in Ghent, Belgium. I started learning Bharatanatyam at the age of four under Guru Chithra Gopinath from Karaikal, Tamil Nadu, where I was trained in the traditional Tanjore style. This early training built my strong foundation in bharatham. Alongside classical dance, I am recently also exploring other styles to grow as a versatile performer. I am currently learning Bollywood dance under my teacher Swapnil in Belgium and I am part of the ABC dance group, performing with them.
