# GIDF — Project Memory

Working memory for the GIDF (Gent India Dance Festival) project. Future Claude sessions should read this before drafting any social or marketing copy.

---

## Brand voice

**Warm, community-y, organizer-from-inside.** Speaks as `we` / `us`, not about the festival as if from outside.

### Markers of the voice

- Specific over abstract — `@swapkebolly on the decks` beats `live music`
- Concrete details — countries / styles / numbers, not poetic verbs
- Casual closing CTAs — `Pull up` / `Save you a spot` / `See you on the floor`
- Permission to say `we` / `us` / `our lineup` — Swapnil is part of the organizing team
- Punctuation is sparse, em-dashes used as breath, not decoration

### AI tells to avoid (do NOT use)

- `✨` openers (or any opener that's just an emoji)
- `One stage. One night.` parallel-structure padding
- `the precision of X, the storytelling of Y, the lyrical grace of Z` triplet-list formula
- Abstract noun-stacks: `tradition, geography, and generations`
- Recycled influencer lines: `Tag a friend who needs to be in the room`
- Em-dashes used as ornament rather than rhythm
- `unforgettable night`, `where magic happens`, `lineup that spans`, `set the tone`
