Your Introduction

Bharatanatyam artist Shilpa Joy, based in the Netherlands, performs "Unfolding: Alarippu" — a piece that translates feeling and meaning into movement, reflecting her lifelong journey to make the internal visible through dance.
About you
A Bharatanatyam artist based in the Netherlands, Shilpa Joy began her journey in the art form at the age of four — a practice that has since grown both into a discipline and a deeply personal way of understanding herself and the world. She trained under Guru Kalamandalam Prasheeja Gopinadh during her formative years, and currently continues her Bharatanatyam training under Guru Shyama Warrier at Natanam Academy, Netherlands, working within a Kalakshetra-influenced approach. At the heart of her practice is a desire to make the internal visible: to translate feeling, character and meaning into movement that resonates.
