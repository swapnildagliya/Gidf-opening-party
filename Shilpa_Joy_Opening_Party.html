About you
A Bharatanatyam artist based in the Netherlands, Shilpa Joy began her journey in the art form at the age of four — a practice that has since grown both into a discipline and a deeply personal way of understanding herself and the world. She trained under Guru Kalamandalam Prasheeja Gopinadh during her formative years, and currently continues her Bharatanatyam training under Guru Shyama Warrier at Natanam Academy, Netherlands, working within a Kalakshetra-influenced approach. At the heart of her practice is a desire to make the internal visible: to translate feeling, character and meaning into movement that resonates.

On the Night — What to Expect

Arrival
Arrive latest by 19:30 if you aren't joining the workshops on Friday — register at the desk, collect your wristband, and you'll be shown to the changing room.
Joining the workshops? You can get ready from 20:00 during the community class.
Drink Coupon
One drink coupon is included — collect it at the registration desk. If you didn't get yours, ask at the desk.
Changing Room
1st Floor, behind the backdrop.
Costume
Bring your costume ready to change into. Go as glam as you like — though bear in mind there's a community class, live band and DJ set after the performances, so you'll want to be comfortable enough to enjoy the night.
Before You Go On
You are 1st in Block 1. You will be called backstage approximately 10 minutes before your block starts. Please be in the changing room and ready by then — do not wander far.
On Stage
You will be introduced by a pre-recorded voiceover (the text on your page). Your track starts automatically after the introduction, once you are on stage. There is no individual sound check — make sure your track is correct before 25 April.
After Performance
Head back to the changing room to change, then join the party as a guest for the rest of the evening.
